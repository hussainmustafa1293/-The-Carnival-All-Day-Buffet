Our Story

A Feast Built On Theatre & Taste

The Carnival began with a simple idea: dining should never be static. Every dish on our floor is finished live — grilled, tossed, or plated in front of you — so the last bite is exactly as good as the first.

Our mission is to bring a premium, all-day buffet experience to Gulberg without losing the warmth of a family table. Ten live stations, one unlimited price, and a dessert island guests linger at long after the main course is done.
